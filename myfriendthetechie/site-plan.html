Overview

Purpose

Think about all of the technology we use in our everyday lives. Do you know anyone who struggles to make sense of any of it? Thatâ€™s where I come in. I help users make sense of and use technology to improve their lives. I help install hardware and software, give tutorials and teach security principles to individual users and businesses who need it most.

Audience

My audience consists of individuals and businesses who have difficulty implementing and using technology. They either don't know where to start or how to use the technology they already have. They may not understand security principles and may need help securing their technology and training on preventative measures.

Content

Home page

[My Friend the Techie is a service to help you make the most of technology for personal use or for your business. Whether you dont know where to start or have questions and need training for what you already have, we can help you leverage technology to make your life easier. We can assess the technology you currently have and help train you how to use it more effectively. We can also recommend new technology for your particular needs. We find out what you would like to accomplish and tailor our services to your specific needs. We can help you with installing hardware and software. We can provide training on how to use both the hardware and software you use so you can get the most benefit from it. We can help you with securing your technology as well. We provide training on using preventative measures along with training on what to do when you have a breach in security.]

Images for the Home page

[Using Technology]

[My friend the Techie can help you understand and use your current technology better. For personal use, we can help with computers, smart phones, smart entertainment, personal home networks and all of the software and hardware used in your home. We can provide training on how to use what you have. We can install and set up any of the items you choose to add to your technology at home. We can help you use technology to make your home more comfortable and secure. We can help you stay connected to loved ones near and far. For business use we can install the hardware and software you need to keep you business running efficiently. We can assess your business needs, and goals and make recommendations based on your circumstances and goals. We can help you grow your business through smart use of software and hardware. We can improve your online presence and implement e-commerce tools to help you grow. ]

Images for the Page 2

[Security in Technology]

[My Friend the Techie is here to answer your security questions and to help you keep your personal and business information safe. We can teach you about security principles you should be using everyday to keep your information secure. We can teach you how to avoid being scammed. We can teach you what to look for and how to identify phishing attempts and more. We can teach you why it is important to use secure passwords. We can assess your current security measures and recommend software and tools to help you decrease your vulnerability and increase your confidence in the safety of your sensitive information. We can also help you develop protocols for when a breach is discovered. ]

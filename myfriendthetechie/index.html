How We Can Help

My Friend the Techie is a service to help you make the most of technology for personal use or for your business. Whether you don't know where to start or have questions and need training for what you already have, we can help you leverage technology to make your life easier. We can assess the technology you currently have and help train you how to use it more effectively. We can also recommend new technology for your particular needs. We find out what you would like to accomplish and tailor our services to your specific needs. We can help you with installing hardware and software. We can provide training on how to use both the hardware and software you use so you can get the most benefit from it. We can help you with securing your technology as well. We provide training on using preventative measures along with training on what to do when you have a breach in security.
